How to export from CMC Invest

  1. Log in to CMC Invest → go to Activity or Transaction History
  2. Click Export / Download CSV (usually top-right)
  3. Save the file, then drag it onto this page

Supported columns: Date, Ticker/Symbol, Type (Buy/Sell), Quantity, Price, Brokerage — exact column names may vary, the app will auto-detect.
